YOUR INVESTMENT SYSTEM — AS A PRODUCT
========================================


WHAT WE'RE BUILDING
=====================

A tool that automates the scanning, scoring, data-pulling, and
monitoring parts of your investment process. It runs your filters
on all stocks above 500 Cr market cap every week, pulls the numbers
you check, flags the setups you look for, and watches your portfolio
for the exit signals you care about.

It does NOT make decisions. Every buy/sell call, every chart pattern
judgment, every conviction call stays with you.

ABOUT THE MARKET PHASE INDICATOR:

Your entire system operates within the broader market context — bull,
sideways, or bear changes how you read signals and size positions.
We want to surface this as a structured indicator at the top, not just
a feeling.

Our PROPOSED criteria (need your input — what do YOU look at to decide
if the market is bull / sideways / bear?):

  BULLISH:
  - Nifty above 52W MA
  - Advance/Decline ratio consistently above 1.5
  - 52W highs significantly exceeding 52W lows
  - FII + DII combined flow positive

  SIDEWAYS:
  - Advance/Decline near 1.0
  - Index range-bound
  - Mixed signals

  BEARISH:
  - Nifty below 52W MA
  - More stocks declining than advancing
  - 52W lows exceeding 52W highs

HOW THE SCORING WORKS:

To rank the stocks that pass your three filters, we propose a scoring
system. This is OUR design — not something you described. The idea is
to give you a quick way to prioritize which stocks to look at first.

  GATES (must pass all three to even appear):
    - Weekly volume >= 5x annual average
    - Price up >= 5%
    - Delivery volume high

  SCORE (each YES adds +1 point, max 8/8):
    1. Delivery % above threshold             +1
    2. Price above 30-week MA                 +1
    3. Price above 52-week MA                 +1
    4. Golden crossover (10W above 30W/52W)   +1
    5. Relative strength positive vs Nifty    +1
    6. Breaking out of 6+ month consolidation +1
    7. Sector index outperforming Nifty       +1
    8. Multiple peers also triggered           +1

WHAT THE SCANNER DOES AND DOESN'T DO:

  DOES: Detect that a stock has been in a price range for 6+ months
  and is now breaking above it. Flags it as a consolidation breakout.

  DOES NOT: Tell you what pattern the consolidation forms. Cup and
  handle, darvas box, inverted H&S, VCP — that's your visual judgment
  when you look at the chart in Screen 3. The system finds the
  candidates. You read the pattern.

Automated: 100%. Runs every Saturday on weekly data. Daily scan optional.

MARKET CONDITION — HOW IT AFFECTS EVERYTHING
=============================================

  BULL MARKET:
    - More stocks trigger signals
    - Higher conviction
    - More sectors participating
    - More aggressive sizing

  SIDEWAYS MARKET:
    - Fewer signals — be selective
    - Maybe only specific sectors working
    - Focus on highest-scoring signals only

  BEAR MARKET:
    - Very few buy signals (that's correct — don't fight it)
    - Cash is a position
    - Focus on exit signals for existing holdings
    - Build watchlist for the recovery
    - The system naturally generates fewer signals —
      that itself is the signal
