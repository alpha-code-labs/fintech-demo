COMPLETE INVESTMENT PROCESS — FLOWCHART
=========================================


PHASE 0: MACRO SCAN (Daily — "Where is the money flowing?")
=============================================================

    Open TradingView
         |
         v
    Global indices (US, Europe, Asia)
         |
         v
    Macro indicators (Dollar index, bond yields, oil)
         |
         v
    Commodities (Gold, crude, metals)
         |
         v
    Indian indices (Nifty, Bank Nifty, Midcap, Smallcap)
         |
         v
    Sector indices (IT, Pharma, Auto, Infra, PSU, etc.)
         |
         v
    FORM A POINT OF VIEW:
    - Is global liquidity supportive or hostile?
    - Where is money flowing — which geography, which asset class?
    - Is the Indian market in a bull, bear, or sideways phase?
    - Which sectors are showing strength or weakness?
         |
         v
    CHECK MARKET DEPTH:
    (a) Advance/Decline ratio — more stocks going up or down?
    (b) 52-week highs vs lows — where is broad strength?
    (c) Large/Mid/Small/Micro — which cohort is closest to
        52-week highs? Which is lagging?
         |
         v
    OUTPUT: "The tide is with us / against us / mixed"
            "Money is flowing into X sector / X asset class"


PHASE 1: LIQUIDITY FILTER (Weekly — "What stocks are showing unusual activity?")
==================================================================================

    Scan CNX 500 (wants to expand to all stocks above 500 Cr market cap)
         |
         v
    FILTER 1: Weekly traded volume >= 5x annual average volume
         |
         ├── NO  → Ignore. No matter how good the company looks.
         |         Not in the pool. Does not exist.
         |
         v YES
         |
    FILTER 2: Price up 5-10% or more alongside that volume spike
         |
         ├── NO  → Check if high volume + price DOWN = bad signal.
         |         Skip or add to "watch for breakdown" list.
         |
         v YES
         |
    FILTER 3: Is delivery volume high? (not just intraday speculation)
         |
         ├── LOW delivery % → Likely traders playing games. Weak signal.
         |                     Watch but don't act.
         |
         v HIGH delivery %
         |
    OUTPUT: Pool of 10-30 stocks with unusual volume + price action
            backed by real buying conviction.


PHASE 2: TECHNICAL CONFIRMATION (Weekly — "Is this a real breakout?")
======================================================================

    Take each stock from Phase 1 pool
         |
         v
    CHECK: Is the stock breaking out of a long consolidation (6+ months)?
         |
         ├── NO consolidation → Might just be a random spike.
         |                       Lower conviction. Still check other signals.
         |
         v YES — breaking out of consolidation
         |
    WHICH PATTERN? (Manual judgment — "part art, part science")
         |
         ├── Cup and Handle?
         ├── Darvas Box?
         ├── Inverted Head and Shoulders?
         ├── Minervini Volatility Contraction Pattern (VCP)?
         ├── Some variation of the above?
         |
         v
    CHECK: Moving average signals
         |
         ├── Is price breaking above 30-week MA?          YES = bullish
         ├── Is price breaking above 52-week MA?          YES = bullish
         ├── Golden crossover (10-week crossing above      YES = momentum
         |   30-week or 52-week)?                          confirmed
         |
         v
    CHECK: Relative strength
         |
         ├── Is this stock outperforming the Nifty?       YES = buyers are
         |                                                  prioritizing this
         |                                                  stock over the market
         |
         v
    *** HUMAN JUDGMENT CHECKPOINT ***
    Visually review charts for each stock.
    Validate pattern quality. This is NOT automated.
    Decide: valid breakout or noise?
         |
         ├── NOT CONVINCED → Drop from list. Move on.
         |
         v CONVINCED
         |
    OUTPUT: Shortlist of maybe 5-10 stocks with confirmed
            technical setups.


PHASE 3: SENTIMENT CHECK ("Is the tide with this stock?")
============================================================

    Take each stock from Phase 2 shortlist
         |
         v
    CHECK: Sector strength
         |
         ├── Are peers in the same sector also showing positive
         |   price action, volume spikes, or breakouts?
         |
         ├── YES → Sector of the cycle may be starting.
         |         Stronger conviction. Check all stocks in sector.
         |
         ├── NO  → This is a single stock story.
         |         Not bad, but less tailwind. Needs stronger
         |         fundamental backing.
         |
         v
    CHECK: Broader market alignment
         |
         ├── Is the overall market supportive? (from Phase 0)
         |
         ├── YES → Tailwind. "When it rains everyone gets wet."
         |
         ├── NO  → Swimming against the tide. Needs VERY strong
         |         signals to proceed. Higher risk.
         |
         v
    OUTPUT: Shortlist with sentiment context —
            "This stock has sector support and market tailwind"
            OR "This is a solo story in a weak market — be careful"


PHASE 4: FUNDAMENTAL FILTER ("WHY is this stock moving?")
============================================================

    Take each stock from Phase 3
         |
         v
    Open Screener.in + Exchange filings + Google/AI for news
         |
         v
    CHECK THE THREE CORE DRIVERS:
         |
         ├── (a) Revenue growth?
         |       Is the company selling more than before?
         |       Compare last quarter vs year-ago quarter.
         |
         ├── (b) Margin growth?
         |       Is the company keeping more of what it sells?
         |       Operating margin or net margin expanding?
         |
         ├── (c) Balance sheet improvement?
         |       Is debt going down? Is working capital improving?
         |       Is ROCE trending up?
         |
         v
    DOES THE REASON FIT ONE OF THE 8 SETUPS?
         |
         ├── 1. Business mix change?
         ├── 2. Management change?
         ├── 3. Sector of the cycle?
         ├── 4. Forced buying or selling?
         ├── 5. Sudden and big earnings surprise?
         ├── 6. Debt reduction?
         ├── 7. Balance sheet improvement?
         ├── 8. Supply disruption?
         |
         ├── FITS A SETUP → Strong conviction.
         |                   The move has fundamental backing.
         |                   The setup also hints at boundary
         |                   conditions (when it might end).
         |
         ├── DOESN'T FIT → Move might be speculation or
         |                  manipulation. Be very cautious.
         |                  Maybe skip.
         |
         v
    *** HUMAN JUDGMENT CHECKPOINT ***
    Apply qualitative checks:
    - Management quality — are these people trustworthy?
    - Is this stock "operated" (manipulated by operators)?
    - What does the network say?
    - AI-assisted risk/reward framework
         |
         ├── NOT CONVINCED → Drop. Move on.
         |
         v CONVINCED
         |
    OUTPUT: Final buy list of maybe 2-5 stocks with full
            technical + sentiment + fundamental alignment.


PHASE 5: POSITION SIZING & ENTRY ("How much to bet?")
=======================================================

    Take final buy list
         |
         v
    PORTFOLIO RULES:
    - Hold 15-25 stocks total
    - Near 15 when aggressive (high conviction market)
    - Near 25 when diversifying (uncertain market)
    - Build position in a few weeks (not all at once)
         |
         v
    *** HUMAN JUDGMENT ***
    - How many signals aligned? (more signals = larger position)
    - Current portfolio composition (already heavy in this sector?)
    - Current market phase (bull = be aggressive, uncertain = be careful)
    - How much cash is available?
         |
         v
    EXECUTE: Buy on broker platform.
    Must hold minimum 6 months (compliance requirement).


PHASE 6: MONITORING & EXIT ("When is the party over?")
========================================================

    For each stock in portfolio — ongoing monitoring
         |
         v
    WATCH FOR EXIT SIGNALS:
         |
         ├── Long upper wicks on weekly candles for 3-4 weeks?
         |   (Price keeps trying to go up but sellers push it down.
         |    A month of this = selling pressure building.)
         |
         ├── Head and Shoulders pattern forming?
         |   (Three peaks, middle one highest. Classic reversal.)
         |
         ├── Price breaking below 30-week or 52-week moving average?
         |   ("This is the most key pattern.")
         |
         ├── Price breaking below a previous support level?
         |   (Floor is broken. Buyers who were defending that
         |    level have given up.)
         |
         ├── Bad news + any of the above technical signals?
         |   (Bad news alone isn't enough. Bad news + technical
         |    breakdown = get out.)
         |
         ├── Significant margin borrowing in the market?
         |   (When people are borrowing heavily to buy stocks,
         |    the market is overleveraged. A small shock can
         |    cause a cascade of forced selling.)
         |
         v
    ALSO CHECK: Has the fundamental setup ended?
         |
         ├── Earnings surprise → was it one-time? Next quarter normal?
         ├── Supply disruption → has supply normalized?
         ├── Sector cycle → is the sector getting exhausted?
         ├── Debt reduction → has it stalled?
         |
         v
    *** HUMAN JUDGMENT CHECKPOINT ***
    This is the hardest part. You're sitting on profits.
    Intermediate consolidation confuses you.
    "Closely watch the pattern being formed
    and lean on the fundamental analysis."
         |
         ├── EXIT SIGNALS PRESENT → Sell. Book profits. Move cash
         |                           to next opportunity.
         |
         ├── NO EXIT SIGNALS → Hold. Let it compound.
         |                      "Let the management compound
         |                       earnings for you."
         |
         v
    CYCLE REPEATS: Cash from exits goes back to Phase 0.
    Start scanning for the next opportunity.


MARKET CONDITION OVERLAY
=========================

All of the above operates WITHIN the broader market context:

    BULL MARKET:
    - System works excellently
    - More stocks triggering signals
    - Higher conviction on positions
    - Can be more aggressive on sizing

    SIDEWAYS MARKET:
    - Fewer signals, be selective
    - Look for "there is always a bull market somewhere"
    - Maybe only specific sectors working
    - Tighter position sizing

    BEAR MARKET:
    - Very few buy signals (this is correct — don't fight it)
    - Focus shifts to: where is the money going?
    - Cash is a position
    - Watch for exit signals on existing holdings
    - Prepare watchlist for the eventual recovery

WHAT WE AUTOMATE vs WHAT STAYS HUMAN
========================================

FULLY AUTOMATED:
- Phase 0: Macro dashboard (indices, breadth, sector strength)
- Phase 1: Volume + price spike screener
- Moving average calculations and crossover detection
- Relative strength calculations
- Market depth (advance/decline, 52w high/low)
- Fundamental data pulls (revenue, margin, debt, ROCE)
- Exit signal monitoring (MA breaks, support breaks)

SEMI-AUTOMATED (system surfaces, human decides):
- Fundamental setup matching (flag which setup might apply)
- Sector correlation (flag if peers are also moving)
- Earnings surprise detection (flag unusual quarterly results)
- News/event scanning with AI

STAYS FULLY HUMAN:
- Chart pattern recognition (consolidation breakouts)
- Management quality assessment
- "Operated stock" identification
- Network checks
- Position sizing decisions
- Final buy/sell calls
