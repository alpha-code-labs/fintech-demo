# UI Walkthrough — 5-Minute Demo Script

Follow these steps to see the full product in action.

---

## Before you start

1. Make sure both backend and frontend are running (see README.md)
2. Open **http://localhost:5173** in your browser
3. Use a desktop/laptop for the best experience (works on mobile too)

---

## Step 1: Global Pulse (30 seconds)

**Where:** You land here when you open the app.

**What to notice:**
- The **CAUTIOUSLY BULLISH** market phase banner at the top — this is the system's read on the overall market
- World indices and commodities in compact cards — green = up, red = down
- Macro indicators: DXY, yields, INR/USD, FII/DII flows
- Indian indices with distance from 52-week high (color coded)
- Market depth: advancing vs declining ratio, 52W highs vs lows
- Sector heatmap: green blocks are strong sectors, sorted by relative strength

**What this proves:** Everything you check across multiple TradingView tabs, on one screen.

---

## Step 2: Signal Scanner (1 minute)

**Where:** Click **"Signal Scanner"** in the left sidebar.

**What to notice:**
- Summary cards at top: 1,247 stocks scanned, 23 triggered, hit rate, top score
- Sort buttons: reorder by Score, Volume, Price Change, or Delivery %
- Each stock row shows price, change, volume vs average, delivery %, and score badge
- **Click any stock row** to expand it — see the 7 technical signals (green check = YES, gray X = NO)
- ABCL at 8/8 is the top pick; PQR Steel at 3/8 with low delivery has a caution note
- Click the arrow icon on ABCL to drill into the deep dive

**What this proves:** The weekly scan runs automatically. Stocks are ranked. You pick which to investigate.

---

## Step 3: Stock Deep Dive (2 minutes)

**Where:** Click the arrow icon next to ABCL, or click **"Deep Dive"** in the sidebar.

**What to notice:**
- Header: stock name, symbol, sector, market cap, score badge (8/8), current price
- Chart area (placeholder) — this is where TradingView charts will go. Key levels shown as chips.
- The yellow note: "YOUR JUDGMENT HERE" — the system doesn't read patterns, you do
- **Technical Signals** panel: volume, delivery, MAs, golden cross, relative strength, consolidation range, breakout level — all with green/gray indicators
- **Sector Context**: Chemical sector RS vs Nifty, plus 3 peers that also triggered. Click any peer to see their deep dive.
- **Fundamentals**: 3 quarters of revenue, margins, profit, debt, ROCE, promoter holding. P/E vs industry P/E.
- **Setup Detection**: 4 setups auto-detected from numbers (earnings surprise, debt reduction, margin expansion, sector cycle). 4 not detected (management change, supply disruption, forced buying, business mix).
- **AI Summary**: one paragraph pulling all data together — facts only, no opinions
- **Action buttons**: Add to Watchlist, Add to Portfolio, Flag for Later, Dismiss
- Bottom note: "YOUR JUDGMENT ON THIS STOCK" — reminder that chart patterns, management quality, conviction are your call

**What this proves:** Everything you pull from Screener.in + TradingView + Google, in one screen. System finds candidates. You read the pattern and decide.

---

## Step 4: Portfolio Monitor (1 minute)

**Where:** Click **"Portfolio"** in the sidebar.

**What to notice:**
- Summary: portfolio value, total P&L, healthy count, attention needed count
- **Health bar** at top: green/orange/red proportional bar
- **Alert section (red)**: STU Auto is already expanded — see 5 exit signals listed (broken below 30W MA, upper wicks, support broken, bad news, auto sector broken). Sell / Hold buttons.
- **Warning section (orange)**: MNO Infra and PQR Chem with early signals. Click to expand.
- **Healthy section (green)**: 11 stocks in a clean list — no issues
- **Sector Concentration**: bar chart showing Chemicals at 27% (orange = potentially concentrated)
- **Market Leverage**: margin borrowing trend with caution note

**What this proves:** Exit signal tracking is automated. You see problems early. The sell decision stays with you.

---

## Step 5: Weekly Briefing (30 seconds)

**Where:** Click **"Briefing"** in the sidebar.

**What to notice:**
- Market phase banner (same as Global Pulse)
- World summary: one paragraph about global markets
- India summary: one paragraph about Indian markets
- Sector highlights: strongest, emerging, weakening
- Top 3 signals with score badges — click any to go to deep dive
- Portfolio alerts: STU Auto (alert) and MNO Infra (warning) — click to go to portfolio
- Quick action buttons at bottom: Open Dashboard, View All Signals, Review Portfolio
- Delivery channels shown: WhatsApp and Email (planned for later)

**What this proves:** Saturday morning, 2-minute read, you know everything. Then click through to act.

---

## Summary

| Screen | Time | What it replaces |
|--------|------|-----------------|
| Global Pulse | 30s | Multiple TradingView tabs, news sites |
| Signal Scanner | 1 min | Manual screening of 1,200+ stocks |
| Stock Deep Dive | 2 min | Screener.in + TradingView + Google per stock |
| Portfolio Monitor | 1 min | Mental tracking of exit signals |
| Weekly Briefing | 30s | Manual summary of the week |

**Total demo time: ~5 minutes**
