GORDON'S ANSWERS — SUMMARY
===========================

SCOPE
-----
- Indian markets only (NSE/BSE)
- International via mutual funds (TDS friction)
- Currently scans CNX 500, wants to expand to anything above 500 Cr market cap
- System works for anything tradeable with price + volume data
- Weekly charts to cut through noise
- Medium to long term holds (6 month minimum — compliance requirement)

- Portfolio: 15-25 stocks (15 when aggressive, 25 when diversifying)

CURRENT TOOLS
-------------
- TradingView: charts, watchlists (preloaded — world indices, Indian indices,
  sector indices, companies by groups, macro, commodities, bonds, portfolio)

--------------
1. Open TradingView
2. Scan: Global charts > Macro > India macro > Commodities > Indian indices >
   Sector indices > CNX 500
3. Form a point of view, shortlist to a watchlist
4. Open Screener.in for fundamental setups
5. Apply human judgment on the shortlist

TIME SPENT
----------
- 1 hour daily
- Few hours on weekends for technical scanning
- Chart scanning + judgment = biggest time sink
- Fundamental analysis shortened with AI

"This is what is happening in the world, this is what is happening in India,
something is happening in these sectors, something is happening in these stocks.
These are the charts, this is the fundamentals, here is the AI generated
risk reward note"
